BROLY!

An "Evil" Character

Broly is a Saiyan who was born with an estimated power level of 10,000 at birth. As a new born baby, he slept in the same room as Goku with whom he shares his date of birth. Due to Goku's constant crying Broly developed a powerful hatred against him and is out for revenge on his lost nap time.
