RADITZ!

An "Evil" Character

Raditz is Goku's older and much taller brother, and the eldest son of Bardock and Gine. He is the first villain in the DragonBall Z series (as well as the first Saiyan seen on screen (apart from Goku himself, of course)). He was one of the four known remaining Saiyans left alive after his home world was destroyed
